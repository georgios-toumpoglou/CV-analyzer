Turn vague experience into compelling bullets

Most resumes suffer from the same problem: they describe what you did, not the value you created. Our AI identifies every bullet point that lacks measurable impact and tells you exactly how to rewrite it — with concrete examples drawn from high-performing CVs in your field.

Whether you're a recent graduate or a seasoned professional, transforming passive descriptions into results-driven statements is the single highest-impact change you can make to your resume.

AI-powered analysis that goes beyond spell-check

CV Analyzer evaluates your resume across five dimensions that actually matter to recruiters: Impact (are your achievements quantified?), Brevity (is every word earning its place?), Style (active voice, no clichés), Skills (are the right keywords present?), and Structure (are essential sections complete?).

Each dimension receives its own score and badge — so you know exactly where to focus your edits, not just that something is "off."

What our CV checker looks for

Here's a snapshot of what the analysis covers:

— Action verbs & impact language: Are you starting bullets with strong, specific verbs?
— Quantified achievements: Numbers, percentages, and outcomes make a difference.
— Brevity & readability: Overly long bullets and repeated phrases hurt scannability.
— Buzzwords & clichés: "Team player", "hard worker" — recruiters tune these out.
— Skills completeness: Hard and soft skills aligned with modern job expectations.
— Structure & sections: Contact info, experience, education — nothing missing.

Frequently Asked Questions

What does CV Analyzer actually check?

It evaluates your resume across five scored dimensions: Impact, Brevity, Style, Skills, and Structure. Each area gets a score out of 100 and specific, actionable feedback — not generic tips.

What file formats are supported?

PDF and DOCX files up to 2 MB. These are the two most common formats for CVs and cover both professionally designed and plain-text resumes. TXT files are not supported to ensure accurate parsing.

Is my CV stored anywhere?

No. Your file is used only for text extraction and analysis, then discarded immediately. We do not store, log, or share any CV content. No account is required.

What is a good overall score?

A score above 75 generally indicates a strong, well-structured resume. Scores between 50–74 suggest clear areas for improvement. Below 50 usually means the resume needs significant rework in multiple areas.

Does it work for CVs in other languages?

The analysis is always returned in English regardless of the CV's language. The AI can read and understand CVs written in Greek, French, German, and other languages — feedback will still be accurate and relevant.

What does the Job Description field do?

If you paste a job posting, the AI will also assess how well your CV matches that specific role — flagging missing keywords, skill gaps, and misaligned experience. It's optional but highly recommended.
